What is Google Analytics? ü§î

Google Analytics is a free web analytics service that tracks and reports website traffic. Think of it as a friendly detective that gathers clues about how visitors interact with your website. It helps you understand things like:

  • Where do your visitors come from? (e.g., Google search, social media, direct)
  • What pages do they visit?
  • How long do they stay?
  • What actions do they take (e.g., make a purchase, fill a form)?
  • And much, much more!

With these insights, you can optimize your website, content, and marketing efforts to better serve your audience and achieve your goals!
